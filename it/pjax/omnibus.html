Omnibus account

Are you a licensed and regulated broker with a large database of active traders? Manage your database more efficiently by consolidating all your client accounts under an omnibus master account. This allows you to keep their individual identities private at the same time.

Caratteristiche principali

More efficient

Trade on behalf of your clients through one account.

Lower fees

Reduce fees associated with deposits and withdrawals.

Privacy

Maintain complete anonymity of your clients.

Come funziona

Create your own white-label application with the omnibus facility, plus all the features supported by our extensive API library. We won’t know who your clients are and vice versa

Create sub-accounts to record transactions you make on behalf of your clients

Customise individual access for clients who wish to trade for themselves

Transfer funds between your omnibus master account and sub-accounts

All deposits and withdrawals are made through your omnibus master account
